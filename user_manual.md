# OneHelp User Manual

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to OneHelp, a platform dedicated to connecting volunteers with organizations to make a positive impact in the community. This user manual will guide you through the features and functionalities of the OneHelp platform.

## 2. User Roles

The OneHelp platform has three main user roles:

*   **Volunteer:** Individuals looking for opportunities to contribute their time and skills.
*   **Organization:** Non-profit or community-based organizations seeking volunteers for their events and projects.
*   **Administrator:** Superusers who manage the platform, verify organizations, and oversee the overall system.

## 3. Getting Started

### 3.1. Registration

To get started with OneHelp, you need to create an account.

#### 3.1.1. Volunteer Registration

1.  Navigate to the registration page.
2.  Select the "Volunteer" role.
3.  Fill in your personal details, including your name, email address, and password.
4.  Click "Register" to create your account.

#### 3.1.2. Organization Registration

1.  Navigate to the registration page.
2.  Select the "Organization" role.
3.  Fill in your organization's details, including the organization name, email address, and password.
4.  Click "Register" to create your organization's account. Your organization will need to be verified by an administrator before you can access all features.

### 3.2. Login

1.  Navigate to the login page.
2.  Enter your registered email address and password.
3.  Click "Login" to access your dashboard.

## 4. Volunteer Dashboard

As a volunteer, you have access to the following features:

### 4.1. Dashboard Overview

Your dashboard provides a summary of your upcoming events, recent messages, and suggested volunteer opportunities.

### 4.2. Managing Your Profile

*   **Update Profile:** Keep your personal information, skills, and interests up-to-date to help organizations find you.
*   **Update Info:** Update your contact information.

### 4.3. Events

*   **View Events:** Browse and search for volunteer opportunities based on your interests, skills, and location.
*   **Register for Events:** Sign up for events you are interested in.

### 4.4. Messaging

*   **Send and Receive Messages:** Communicate directly with organizations regarding volunteer opportunities.

### 4.5. Account Management

*   **Change Password:** Update your account password.
*   **Deactivate Account:** You can choose to deactivate your account if you no longer wish to use the platform.

## 5. Organization Dashboard

As an organization, you have access to the following features:

### 5.1. Dashboard Overview

Your dashboard provides a summary of your active events, the number of applications received, and key analytics.

### 5.2. Event Management

*   **Create Events:** Post new volunteer opportunities, including event details, required skills, and location.
*   **Manage Events:** View, edit, or delete your existing events.

### 53. Application Management

*   **View Applications:** Review applications from volunteers who have registered for your events.
*   **Update Application Status:** Accept or decline volunteer applications.

### 5.4. Analytics

*   **View Analytics:** Track the number of volunteers, event attendance, and other key metrics to measure your impact.

### 5.5. Messaging

*   **Send and Receive Messages:** Communicate with volunteers who have applied for your events.

## 6. Administrator Dashboard

The administrator dashboard is used for managing the OneHelp platform.

*   **User Management:** Manage volunteer and organization accounts.
*   **Organization Verification:** Review and approve new organization registrations.
*   **Platform Analytics:** Monitor the overall health and growth of the platform.
